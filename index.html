Studying Abroad in Ireland!

Hi! I created this site to keep my family and friends updated on my study abroad adventures. I'm also treating this as an archive of photos and memories from my time abroad. My semester abroad is my first time travelling outside of the US and I want to remember it all. I will be studying at Dublin City University and living in Dublin, Ireland, with (hopefullly) some trips outside of Ireland along the way! I will put all the juicy details of what I'm up to on here. Photos will probably be posted more frequently to my instagram (because I can do so from my phone and not have to whip out my mac and log into github). Thanks!
